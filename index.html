ℹ️ How This Works

This calculator uses multiplicative filtering — each preference you select reduces the total pool of Indian men by the estimated proportion who meet that criterion. The filters are applied independently (as if each trait were statistically unrelated), which gives a rough estimate of how selective your combined preferences are.

The demographic assumptions used here are simplified approximations based on publicly available data from the Census of India, NFHS surveys, and other demographic studies. They are meant to give you a feel for relative rarity — not an exact count.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This is a demo project for entertainment and educational purposes only. The data model is illustrative and not suitable for real matchmaking decisions. Results should not be interpreted as precise statistics. No personal data is collected or stored. The assumptions can be replaced with real datasets for greater accuracy.
